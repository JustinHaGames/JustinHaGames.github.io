Axolotl

An interactive narrative game where patience unlocks different perspectives of a story about a man and an axolotl.

Developed as a final project - Spring 2017

Writer and Designer | Made in Unity with John Sampson, Julian Cordero, & Rahul Joshi


Axolotl explores the idea that player patience and reflection deserves a deeper story. As you play the game, be patient and discover differences to unlock new parts of the story.
