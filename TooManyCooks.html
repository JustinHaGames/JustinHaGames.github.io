Too Many Cooks

A 4-player strategy card/board game where everyone works together to complete dishes, but only the player to finish the recipe wins!

Developed as a final project - Fall 2017

Lead Game Designer | Made in Unity with Rahul Joshi, Mattias Lambert, and Max McHarris

Players must try to outsmart their opponents by setting up plans, buying new abilities, and preventing other players from being crowned the best cook!
