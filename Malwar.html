Mal*War

A two player game where the key to survival lies in the hands of the other player.

One player plays on the keyboard and is responsible for destroying the viruses and gaining material for the other player.

The second player plays on the mouse and helps the other player by dropping different crates that provide health, bullets, or protection.

Developed as a final project - Spring 2016

Designer, Coder(Enemy and spawning), and 3D Modeler | Made in Unity with Kendrick Tse and Charles Yu
